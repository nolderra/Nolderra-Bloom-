🌿 Bloom — Privacy Policy

By Nolderra  |  Special Needs Parenting Companion

📅 Last updated: May 30, 2026

Nolderra ("we," "us," or "our") built the Bloom app to support caregivers of children with special needs. We take the privacy of your family seriously — especially because this app may be used to record sensitive health-related information about a child. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Bloom may collect the following types of information:

  • Child Profile Data: Name, age, diagnoses, and therapist information you enter voluntarily.
  • Routine & Behavior Logs: Daily routines, behavior incidents, triggers, interventions, moods, and notes you record.
  • ABA Tracking Data: Skill progress entries and therapy goal information.
  • AI Chat Data: Messages you send to Bloom AI. These are sent to the Anthropic API to generate responses and are not stored by Nolderra.
  • Device Information: Basic device data for app stability (OS version, crash logs). No personally identifiable device data is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Power the app's core features: routines, behavior logging, insights, and AI chat.
  • Generate insights and reports you can share with your care team.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All personal data you enter in Bloom is stored locally on your device using encrypted secure storage. We do not operate a cloud database storing your child's information unless you opt into a future cloud sync feature (which will be clearly disclosed).

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data on-device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Bloom is designed for use by caregivers and parents, not by children directly. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will delete it.

Information about a child (such as their name, age, and behavior data) is entered by the adult caregiver. This information is treated with the highest level of care and is not used for any advertising or profiling purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Bloom uses the following third-party service:

  • Anthropic Claude API: Powers the Bloom AI chat feature. When you send a message to Bloom AI, that message is transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data. We recommend not including full names or identifying details when using the AI chat. Learn more at anthropic.com/privacy.

6. Data Retention

Your data lives on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Delete My Data" option in Settings. We retain no server-side copies of your personal data under the current app architecture.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data stored in the app (viewable directly in the app).
  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app or using the delete option in Settings.
  • Request information about how your data is used by contacting us below.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Please contact us to exercise those rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
